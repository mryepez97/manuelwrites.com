About Me

I am Manuel Yepez, a father to two beautiful children and a husband to a selfless and loving wife. I am also a writer and deep thinker that is constantly exploring the meaning of life while pursuing the answers of our own purpose and existence.

My curiosity has always been drawn toward the deeper questions of our existence: What gives life meaning? How do our beliefs shape our choices? What can history teach us about ourselves?

Through my essays, I explore philosophy, history, science, personal growth, and the human experience. Through my books, I encourage readers to review that life is fragile and our existence is a miracle. Let us become the best version of our tomorrow, today.

Why I Write

Writing is a way for me to examine the world, organize ideas, and explore questions that have fascinated humanity for centuries.

Whether through a philosophical essay, a self-improvement book, or a fictional story, I hope my work encourages readers to think deeply, grow personally, and see the world from new perspectives.

"The liberty of speaking and writing guards our other liberties." -Thomas Jefferson

My Approach

I believe that ideas matter. The stories we tell, the beliefs we hold, and the lessons we learn from history influence the people we become and the societies we create.

This website is a place where I share my writing, ongoing projects, and reflections on the questions that continue to inspire me.
